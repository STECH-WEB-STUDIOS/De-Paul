ABOUT US

The De Paul Linguistic Coaching Centre (DPLCC) is a language institute run by Vincentian Fathers. Rev. Fr. Tomson Pananal V.C. founded it on October 3, 2022. It is located in Nedumkunnam, Mulayamveli road, Kottayam district, Kerala. This institute is distinctive in the way it incorporates a campus and a hostel under one roof. We primarily instruct German. Our primary objective is to provide a successful career and excellent faculty from India and abroad. De Paul assists students in achieving long-term work or academic success through continuous learning and test preparation. Our concerted effort is to improve their performance and support them in accomplishing their primary goals.

FR TOMSON GEORGE V.C

Director

Fr. Tomson George V.C. took the initiative to establish the first language institute in Nedumkunnam, with the goal of providing language classes to help people from ordinary families achieve their overseas dreams at a lower cost. He has made numerous contributions to the field of education by establishing and developing various institutions to assist children from middle-class families.

De Paul LCC intends to expand its consulting services in order to assist people who want to study or work abroad. DPLCC will undoubtedly provide the best coaching and consulting services to assist students in meeting their objectives.
